Featured Automation & Machine Build Projects

Compact UV Curing System for Offset Press (Packaging Automation)

The Challenge: Integrate a powerful UV curing system into a high-speed offset printing press (relevant to packaging sector), a task made incredibly difficult by an extremely constrained physical space and the requirement to operate within a 2mm tolerance of fast-moving machine components.

My Leadership & Contribution: As a hands-on leader, I engineered a solution from first principles. I used 3D printing to rapidly prototype and test several iterations of a custom, ultra-compact, water-cooled LED module housing. I also designed and fabricated bespoke hydraulic connections to fit the tight space. This process of hands-on iteration and custom fabrication was the key to delivering a functional system that solved a critical production bottleneck for the client, demonstrating my ability to turn bold ideas into reliable machines under tight deadlines and integrate precision components in highly constrained mechanical environments.

Autonomous UVC Disinfection Robot (High-Performance Automation)

The Challenge: Develop a fully autonomous robotic system for pathogen disinfection in critical environments during the pandemic, requiring a solution that was both effective and safe for remote operation, and compliant with pharmaceutical standards.

My Leadership & Contribution: As development leader, I owned the project from concept to commissioning. I personally designed the complete mechatronic system, integrating an Omron AMR platform with custom sensor arrays (LIDAR, vision) and Arduino-based controls. Handled 3D modeling, weight distribution analysis, and initial firmware programming for sensor and safety system integration. Managed the project's most critical technical constraint: balancing the significant weight of the power sources against the UVC emitters' high energy demands, all while ensuring robust safety interlocks and pharmaceutical compliance. This project showcases my ability to lead complex R&D projects and deliver high-performance automation systems.

UV Curing System for Metal Printing (CMP - Robust Machine Build)

The Challenge: Create a high-performance UV curing system for an industrial metal printing line that required precise, real-time control over multiple physical parameters, including temperature, hydraulic flow, and electrical load, while ensuring compliance with stringent safety standards.

My Leadership & Contribution: My role was to architect and build the entire electromechanical control system from the ground up. I executed the full mechanical design in SolidWorks and the electrical schematics in EPLAN. I then personally programmed the Siemens PLC and HMI from scratch, developing a robust control logic that included custom PID loops for precise LED temperature management, hydraulic flow control for the cooling system, and variable current control for the panel fans—all while ensuring the final system was fully compliant with NR-12 safety standards. This project demonstrates my expertise in robust machine build, system integration, and control design for high-performance industrial applications.

Punch-Cutting System for Citroën C3 XTR Bumper (Special Purpose Machinery)

The Challenge: Design a precision punch-cutting machine for mounting holes in large, externally visible plastic bumpers, requiring flawless surface finish, exact positioning, and high repeatability.

My Leadership & Contribution: I led the full mechanical design using CATIA and Siemens NX, collaborating closely with the design studio to translate Class-A surfaces into manufacturable geometry. Engineered punch-and-die tools actuated by hydraulic cylinders, with pneumatic positioning systems. Integrated light curtains and sensors for operator safety. The solution was approved, installed on the PSA production line, and adapted for other models, demonstrating my ability to deliver high-precision, repeatable special purpose machinery from concept to production.
